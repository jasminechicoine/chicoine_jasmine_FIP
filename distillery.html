Our Distillery

We managed to install a state of the art solar-plus-battery storag system capable of harnessing our powerful sun to provide 25% of their overall power needs. We're also developing a C02 reclamation center that will allow them to syphon off gas produced naturally during fermentation for use later in the brewing process.

Sustainability

Our Sustainability Promise

When Mother Nature gives you a gift, you must protect it. At Zima, we are committed to sustainability and reducing our environmental impact. We promise to use 100% recyclable materials for our cans and packaging, reduce our water usage during production, and source our ingredients from suppliers who prioritize sustainable practices.
